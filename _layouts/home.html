Hello, I'm a/an

I'm Yubin, currently a visiting student/researcher at Personal Robots Group, MIT Media Lab.
My research objective and interest lies in the area of human-robot interaction.
Specifically, I'm interested in studying affect recognition for child early literacy learning and developing dialogue system for emotional wellness and medication support for older adults.

Projects

My experience has been focused on developing sociable robots that can perceive and understand social and affective exchanges in human interactions in an intuitive and reciprocal manner. Specifically, I am interested in developing sociable robots that can personalize their interactions with humans in the wild for an extended period of time.
