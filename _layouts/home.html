Hello, I'm a/an

I'm Yubin, currently a visiting student/researcher at Personal Robots Group, Media Lab.
My research objective and interest lies in the area of human-robot interaction.
Specifically, I'm interested in studying affect recognition for child early literacy learning and developing dialogue system for emotional wellness and medication support for older adults.

Projects

jibo can help children who need extra attention due to learning difficulties or disorders. Children are more eager to emotionally connect and be physically activated by a social robot. For any school subject, including foreign language learning, jibo can be the nexus between students and teachers, supporting the learning process with gamification strategies that keep students motivated. Children can also learn how to program interactions with jibo themselves using the SCRATCH language, thanks to the Be a Maker © application.
